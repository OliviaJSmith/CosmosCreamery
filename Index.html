Cosmo's Creamery is named after a beloved family cat who was named after the flower (not Cosmo Kramer or the outer space kind - though I am fine with both those associations!). The story starts before I (Hi, Olivia the owner here!) was even born with my dad who made it his mission to make the best homemade chocolate ice cream you had ever had. I took my dad's recipe with me when I moved to Spokane, WA in 2020, and now, after many years of making it for family, friends, and coworkers, it's time to share it with more people!
